Egg Fried Rice

Description

Egg fried rice. Yes, the egg fried this rice, haha very funny. Simple, fast, arguably-healthy dish that's perfect if you're single and have no dependents or if you need to feed a family. Just change the quantities and use a bigger wok.

Ingredients

  • Cold rice
  • Eggs
  • Frozen Vegetables
  • Any other protein (like spam or chiness sausage)
  • Soy sauce
  • Onion, garlic, herbs...

Steps

  1. Warm up frozen veggies
  2. Fry the onion and garlic in wok until fragant
  3. Add rice in wok
  4. Add veggie, protein, soy sauce
  5. Add mixed eggs
  6. Stir fry until you think it's ready. (usally when the rice is dry but not burnt )
